How to Play

  • You start at the bottom-left corner of a 5×5 grid.
  • Each turn, roll the 1-10 digital dice.
  • Click a neighboring cell. If your roll matches or beats its hidden requirement, you reveal it and step in.
  • Hawkins Blocks are safe, Upside Down Blocks deal −5 HP.
  • Watch out! Hidden Demogorgons deal −10 HP, and the Mind Flayer deals −15 HP!
  • Power-ups (Usable after move 1): Psychic power reveals all neighboring tiles instantly. Flamethrower burns an adjacent hidden box so it can be crossed without requiring a dice roll.
  • Escape through the hidden Gate with more HP than Vecna to win. He starts at 5 HP and gains +1 every second!
