How to Play

  • You and the Bot each pick a number from 0 to 100.
  • The average of both is multiplied by 0.8.
  • That result is the Spider Number.
  • Whoever is closest to it wins the round.
  • Loser loses a ❤️. First to 0 hearts loses.
